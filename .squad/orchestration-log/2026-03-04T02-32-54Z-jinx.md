## Design Rationale

- **Consistency:** Matches eyebrow_offset and nose_offset architecture (orthogonal feature state)
- **Non-breaking:** Existing expression mouth shapes continue working when viseme override inactive
- **Composable:** Speech can layer with any expression for emotional + speech combinations
- **Recording-ready:** Commands capture to timeline like gaze/eyebrow/nose for playback
- **Projection-safe:** All shapes use pure white (255,255,255) on pure black (0,0,0) maintaining 21:1 contrast
